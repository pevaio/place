dream diary: entry 1

i am in hell.

here is cold and everything is grey. there are no souls being tortured nor any painful screams. in fact, there is no sound at all, since i can hear my own breathing. there is a strong, bluish fog covering my field of view.

thus, i walk, hearing the echo of my footsteps. i go on walking for some hundred meters until i hear a deep roar. i, then, naively try to stab myself, perhaps in order to flee. to no surprise, it does not work, since i am already dead.

then, i see the source of the roar tearing through the fog. it is a pitch-black, humanoid slender creature which had three eyes: one in its face and one in each hand. when i gaze at its upper eye, i instantly feel a pressure in my whole body, so strong that it makes me collapse in seconds.

i wake up.
