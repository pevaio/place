why i'm despotifying

to despotify: to quit spotify, the most popular music streaming platform in spite of a better alternative, be it another streaming service, local playing or even physical media.

first of all, i would like to clarify some information about this company's founder and ex-ceo: daniel ek. he is neither some kind of audiophile or musician. despite not having completed university, it's clear that ek is a marketer, since he has worked for a plethora of companies in the marketing and communication departments, but never in an audio-related role. besides that, he also founded another company: advertigo, whose name is quite self-explanatory. furthermore, he is also involved in businness with a military technology startup called helsing, which mainly manufactures combat drones.

secondly, spotify's payments to artists are extraordinarily low, ranging from approximately US$0.003 to US$0.005 per stream. this is because a great part of the revenue goes to the music labels, circa 61%. meanwhile, only 22% of this money is directed to the artists, songwriters and publishers. it is later subdivided between these agents, with 9% going to the artists.

therefore, the best things to do, at least for me are either 1) to download songs through "copyleft" means, while supporting their artists through official merch or concerts and 2) to buy their tracks digitally through websites like bandcamp. although you can also simply migrate to a better-paying streaming platform.

thanks for the read!
